## Tech Gift Selection Guide

**Ask these questions:**
1. **Apple or Android?** - Compatibility matters
2. **Gamer?** - Consider Switch, headset, or controller
3. **Work from home?** - Desk accessories, webcam, mouse
4. **Traveler?** - Power bank, earbuds, Kindle
5. **Smart home curious?** - Echo Dot or Hue lights

**When in doubt:** AirPods or a nice power bank. Everyone needs them.
