## Gifting Strategy

When shopping on a budget:
1. **Buy quality over quantity** - One $25 item beats five $5 items
2. **Stick to consumables** - Nice food, candles, and skincare always work
3. **Think practical** - Useful beats decorative every time
4. **Add nice packaging** - A $15 gift looks expensive in good wrapping
